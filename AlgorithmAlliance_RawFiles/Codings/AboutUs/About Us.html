About Us

About Urban Oasis

Urban Oasis Resort Tarlac was founded by Modesto Ordoñez. It is a hotel resort, sole proprietorship company which means the owner runs the business himself. Originally, Urban Oasis Resort Tarlac was named Urban Oasis and was changed to Urban Oasis Resort Tarlac on June 28, 2022. Urban Oasis Resort Tarlac has been welcoming guests since June 14, 2022. The resort has a Facebook page that was created on April 8, 2021, serves as a platform for guests to stay updated to latest promotions, updates and feedback on their experiences. The resort is located in Kaldera St. Barangay Culipat Road, Tarlac.

About the Resort

Urban Oasis Resort, Tarlac provides a range services and amenities to help make your stay more comfortable. Stay connected throughout your stay with free internet access provided. Parking is provided free of charge for guests. You'll have no problem planning out your days and transportation needs with front desk services including concierge service and luggage storage. The resort's daily housekeeping will make staying in a great choice. You can also get small travel items and sundries at the convenience stores without having to leave the Urban Oasis Resort, Tarlac. Smoking may only take place in restricted designated areas. Guests are guaranteed a comfortable stay while at Urban Oasis Resort, Tarlac. To enhance your stay, some rooms at the resort come with linen service, blackout curtains and air conditioning. The specially-designed rooms at Urban Oasis Resort, Tarlac offer a layout option like a balcony or terrace. You may be assured to know that a refrigerator, a coffee or tea maker, bottled water, instant coffee and mini bar are available in some select rooms. You can stay fresh and clean with toiletries, bathrobes and towels provided in some of the guest bathrooms.
